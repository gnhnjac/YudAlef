What is death run?

Death run is a game where there are runners and deaths. The runners need to escape from the death's traps and finish the route. The death needs to kill them  with his traps.

But our map is different from the other death runs.

Who are we?

We are some players that play minecraft and like to create maps so others can enjoy playing them.
